About our company

Enstructura Pvt. Ltd. began its journey in 2013 as a proprietary practice under the name Enstrectura Consultants. To broaden our reach and deliver comprehensive civil engineering solutions, the practice was incorporated as a private limited company in 2017 by a team of seasoned professionals committed to providing innovative and state-of-the-art consultancy services.

Enstructrua Consultants (Pvt.) Ltd., having 30+ post graduated engineers and about 15+ Cad engineers to deliverables in time. Company is also having NABL certified NDT testing facility and in-house civil engineering material testing Lab.

Our History

The experts at our company bring individual experience ranging from 20 to 40 years across various domains of civil and structural engineering. Their professional journeys include tenures at renowned organizations such as the Torsteel Research Foundation in India (TRFI), Civilaid, and Bureau Veritas India Ltd. They were mentored by the late Dr. C.S. Vishwanatha, one of the most respected structural consultants of his time.

Additionally, our directors have worked with esteemed organizations such as KIOCL, JSW Steel, Bremholding Berhad (Malaysia), and Vaswani Developers, gaining extensive expertise in the design and execution of industrial structures and high-rise buildings.

Our Mission

Our mission is to combine all applicable skills to resolve any critical problems in civil engineering and obtain solutions that are functionally sound, economically feasible, and practically applicable.

We strive to deliver technical excellence while upholding the highest standards of ethics and integrity in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to deliver innovative, sustainable, reliable solutions in civil engineering that shape the future of infrastructure. We strive to continuously evolve through knowledge, research, and ethical practices, making a meaningful impact in the built environment.
