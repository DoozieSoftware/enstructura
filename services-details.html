structural Design Consultancy

Our Design Division is comprised of a team of highly qualified post-graduate engineers specializing in Structural Engineering. The division is equipped with licensed and industry-standard structural analysis and design software, including ETABS, SAFE, STAAD.Pro, CYPE, and RCDC, enabling us to deliver accurate and efficient structural solutions.

We also utilize CAD software for precise detailing. The use of Revit and Sketch-Up enhances the detailing process, providing clearer visualizations that improve understanding and communication at the construction site. Our detailing engineers bring extensive experience in preparing structural drawings with a strong focus on constructability and ease of execution at the site. This ensures our designs are not only technically sound but also practical to implement.

Structural Design

  • Design Of Residential, Commercial, educational and Hospital buildings.
  • Design of industrial structures, PEB structures, pipe rack structures, Belt conveyors supporting systems.
  • Design of Silos and Bunkers
  • Design of Irrigation structures, Ground supported water reservoirs and Overhead water tanks for drinking water supply schemes.
  • Design of water treatment plants and Waste water treatment plants.
